Besel pro.activ

We took over the village of Zonhoven with Becel pro.activ, engaging the villagers in a 4 week cholesterol lowering activation program. With daily activities to educate, support and motivate them, 77% lowered their cholesterol. The campaign was broadcasted every day in prime time, was followed by weekly magazines and reached more than 1 million Belgians on youtube. The participating villagers are still active brand ambassadors.
