STORY TRAVEL COMPANION

The story of SPICE is a story about creating engaging stories and impactful personalized interactions with consumers, to grow brands and businesses sustainably.

Spice trade has always been a job for pioneers. Brave men discovering new horizons returned with both failures and successes, but always with new experiences. They built on learnings to build an economy.

That’s why SPICE is a story of Startup Projects, pioneering and prototyping new business ideas with a can do mentality. Some will fail, some will succeed, but all will build expertise towards next endeavours.
